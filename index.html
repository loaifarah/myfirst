where did the idea came from?

Our family has always faced challenges in sourcing quality firewood for our fireplaces. Recognizing this issue, my friend Loai Farah and I, Melad Nicola, decided to create a project aimed at assisting not only our relatives but also anyone else who encounters the same problem. We are committed to providing a reliable solution that ensures access to high-quality firewood for everyone.
